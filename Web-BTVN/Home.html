Features

Children between the age of 6 and 12 years old act as mascots for the Chelsea team before each home and away game. A waiting list operates for children to become mascots. New names are added in the order in which applications are received, we cannot give a definite wait time. Children have to be Junior members at the time of being a mascot. The length of the waiting list should be considered when making an application. For instance, a 10-year-old would not currently be added as the child will not reach the top of the list before reaching the age limit. .
